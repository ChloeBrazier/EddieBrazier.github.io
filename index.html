Ship of Shadows is a puzzle-platformer based on the mysterious behaviors of objects under the ship's lights. The player must engage with three kinds of light to make it to the top of the ship and uncover the mystery as to how they got there.

Play Online

My Contributions:

  • Designed the “unequipped” and ‘candle” lighting mechanics
  • Implemented a finite state machine for the player
  • Used special lighting cookies to create lighting that fit the retro graphical style

The A-maze-ing marionette was a game jam game made for the RIT Game Dev Club's 2019 Day of the Dead Jam. You play as a puppetmaster who controls a marionette through a maze full of traps! Be careful, or your strings may be snapped. Reaching the end of the maze won't be easy.

Download Here

My Contributions:

  • Coded the boulder and pitfall traps
  • Textured assets
  • Created the live title screen scene
